Reading

What I read on a daily basis dramatically influences my storytelling. With this in mind, tracking on how much I read and curate across my network is important.

My reading and curating networks are pretty refined and localized to a couple locations.

Questions:

  • How many blog posts did I read?
  • How many blog posts did I curate?

Services:

  • Google Reader
  • Pinboard

The number of posts I scanned and read is available manually within Google Reader.

Everything I curate is run through pinboard. If I favorite in Twitter or Google Reader it goes to Pinboard.

I use a pinboard bookmarklet to curate things I find manually. So everything curated runs through Pinboard.
